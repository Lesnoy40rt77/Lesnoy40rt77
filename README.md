## About me

I’m a backend-focused developer interested in **real products**, **infrastructure**, and the parts of software engineering that begin after a nice demo:  
deployment, CI, API architecture, reliability, and shipping things that actually work.

Right now my main focus is **Ventus Iter** — a product for travelers, outdoor enthusiasts, and families who need route decisions with weather-aware context.  
Inside the project I work on **backend/API**, **staging deployment**, **CI**, and the technical glue that keeps the product moving toward a real MVP.

I study **Applied Informatics at Russian State Hydrometeorological University** and I’m open to **strong backend tasks**, **product teams**, and **internship opportunities**.
